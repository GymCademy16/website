About Us

A Different Approach to Child Development

GymCademy is a child therapy centre offering sessions for typical and special needs children aged 1-15 years.

What sets us apart is our commitment to knowledge sharing. Our primary goal is to equip parents with the skills to provide early intervention at home, creating a supportive environment for continuous development.
